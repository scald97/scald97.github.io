I'M serena howard

I am Serena Howard. As a person who likes to find answers and fix problems as well as take on challenges software development has always been something I found interesting but thought was too out of my league. However, after 10 years of thriving but not feeling fulfilled in customer service I knew that I needed to do something I was more passionate about. I first started learning how to code taking a free course offered by Harvard as a CS50 class. It was just an introduction to the many languages used by developers and engineers but I was hooked. After beginning my research on programs and with a few friends already working in the field I had a few suggestions on how to further my education and learn to code I applied to We Can Code IT. Five months of hard work later I am only begginning this journey but I am so excited to keep learning and growing adding to the number of women in tech!
